Antiseptic Products

Antiseptic Cream

Active Ingredients: Silver sulphadiazine + Trimethoprim

Indications: Broad spectrum topical cream for all types of infections. Supports healing of open wounds.

Side Effects: Hypersensitivity for sulpha drugs (Rare).

Contraindications: No contraindications.

Antiseptic Topical Lotion

Active Ingredients: Sulphamethoxazole + Trimethoprim, Rhubarb extract, Sassura Costus extract

Indications: Broad spectrum topical antiseptic lotion. Decreases inflammation and supports wound healing.

Side Effects: Hypersensitivity for sulpha drugs (Rare).

Contraindications: No contraindications.
