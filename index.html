Antiseptic Lotion

Active Ingredients:

Sulphamethoxazole + Trimethoprim, Rhubarb extract, Sassura Costus extract

Indications:

Broad spectrum topical antiseptic lotion. Decreases inflammation and supports wound healing.

Side Effects:

Hypersensitivity for sulpha drugs (Rare).

Contraindications:

No contraindications.
